Partners & funders

We don't do this alone.

Since 2018, Hope Training Academy has kept tech education within reach — free for those who qualify — thanks to funders, universities, employers and community anchors who believe in hands-on hope across Indiana.

Funders & grants

The support behind our students.

Indianapolis Urban League

A leading client and partner — including the IIAQ workforce grant, with deep insight into the students and communities we serve.

Thomas P. Miller & Associates

Workforce strategy and grant partnership behind HTA's programs and formal program design.

CyberHope AI

HTA's biggest sponsor — funding our AI-forward curriculum and building the technology behind this platform.

Higher education

Universities that go hands-on with us.

IU Luddy School (IUPUI)

Co-built our virtual-reality "build a computer" course for certification classes — immersive, repeatable hands-on learning.

Indiana Wesleyan University

A higher-education partner extending hands-on tech pathways for Hoosiers.

Marian University

Partnering to broaden access to tech education and apprenticeship pathways.

Martin University

Indianapolis's own historically Black university — a longtime partner in our story.

Community & alignment

Rooted where students are.

Shepherd Community Center

A community anchor on the near-eastside and part of HTA's history of meeting students where they are.

Indiana CSA

Aligned with Indiana's Computer Science Aspirations pathways for IT/CS and STEM careers.

Local community centers

Neighborhood partners across Indianapolis who help us reach the people who need us most.
