Our historic home

A training school then. A training academy now.

Historic Emmerich Manual Training School at 525 S Meridian St. Photo courtesy of Union 525.

There's a poetry to where we teach. Our home at 525 South Meridian opened in 1895 as the Emmerich Manual Training School — built to teach Hoosiers a trade with their hands. More than a century later, HTA does the very same thing within the same walls: hands-on training for the careers of today.

  • Teaching hands-on trades on this site since 1888
  • Longtime home of the Emmerich Manual Training High School
  • Reborn as Union Campus in 2016 — a 210,000 sq ft downtown tech hub
